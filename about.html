OUR STORY

We are a family-owned business that honors the memory of our beloved father and grandfather, Ingo.

We opened our doors in 2022, with the vision of creating a simple coffee bar that serves high-quality, wholesome and delicious food and drinks. We are passionate about using fresh ingredients from local farms and suppliers, such as stone ground flour, olive oil and marzipan. Whether you are looking for a hot or cold beverage, a pastry, a sandwich or a salad, we have something for you. You can also enjoy our free Wi-Fi and our cozy atmosphere, where you can relax and chat with friends or family. We also offer a variety of products for you to take home or gift to someone special. Our team is friendly, professional and eager to serve you. Come and visit us today and see why we are more than just a coffee bar. We are a community

With love, Beatrix Pettit and the Ingos Coffee Bar team
